About Us





Who Are We?



The story of the creators of Mental Well Map is a testament to the transformative power of friendship, empathy, and the strength found in vulnerability. It all began when one of our own, a close friend, silently battled a series of mental health challenges. Despite our strong friendship, the stigma surrounding mental health had created a barrier, and he felt ashamed to confide in anyone. However, our unwavering support and insistence eventually broke through those walls. As he began to share his struggles, we delved into the world of mental health, learning, and empathizing with his journey. Witnessing the profound impact that support and understanding can have, we decided to channel our experiences into creating MentalWellMap. Our mission was clear: to guide and assist the youth in their mental health journey, making a meaningful difference in the world and fostering a movement where strength is found in speaking out and extending a hand of support.



Our Mission

"At Mental Well Map, our mission is to be a guiding light for young people. We want to break the silence around mental health and show that it's okay to talk about it. Our goal is to create a safe place where you can find support and feel better. We're here to help you navigate your feelings and be strong together. We're not just a website; we're a movement. Let's change the way we think about mental health. We believe that by talking and supporting each other, we can find hope and strength in ourselves."
