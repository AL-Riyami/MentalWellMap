Subscription Plans




Basic Plan

Free

This plan is perfect for those who want to explore the basic features of Mental Well Map without any cost. It includes access to limited resources and community support. It's an excellent starting point for users who are curious about our platform.

  • - Access to basic educational content
  • - Participation in community discussions
  • - Limited access to the therapist directory
  • - Email support during business hours
  • - Access to the FAQ section for common queries

Premium Plan

$19.99/month

The Premium Plan is designed for users who want to unlock the full potential of Mental Well Map. It offers comprehensive access to educational content, therapist listings, and premium support. With this plan, users can take their mental well-being to the next level.

  • - Full access to all educational content and resources
  • - Unlimited engagement in community discussions
  • - Access to a wide range of mental health professionals and their profiles
  • - Priority email support with faster response times
  • - Exclusive content and webinars
  • - 24/7 access to support

Professional Plan

$29.99/month

The Professional Plan is tailored for mental health professionals and experts who want to leverage Mental Well Map's resources for their practice. It provides advanced tools and dedicated support to enhance their services and reach a wider audience.

  • - All premium features
  • - Enhanced profile visibility in the therapist directory
  • - Integration with appointment scheduling and teletherapy tools
  • - Dedicated account manager for personalized assistance
  • - Access to advanced analytics and insights
  • - Premium listing in our exclusive therapist directory
